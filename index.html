About

I'm a music producer, sound engineer, and instrumentalist. I work with the creation, recording, and finalization of music — both original projects and collaborations with other artists. I have experience in improvisation, experimental electronics, and music production, always aiming to integrate sound, creativity, and technology.

Músico, Compositor, Engenheiro e Produtor Musical

I performed as an interpreter on the track Motivos / Patuá / Queda Livre (Navegantes Vol. 2), worked as the recording engineer on the album The Trip (Magro Merlin & The Unruly), and produced À frente da percepção (MIX-FOF-CV-BANG!). In 2023, I participated in the Unicamp Improvisation Collective, contributing to projects such as Antinomias e Autonomia and IMPROCINE: Improvised Music in Silent Film. I also develop research and practical projects that explore sound in a broad way — including sound synthesis, musical accessibility, and interactive composition.
